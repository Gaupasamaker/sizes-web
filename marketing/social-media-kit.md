## AI Video Prompts (Luma/Runway/Sora)

### Lifestyle Concept: "The Smart Shopper"
> **Prompt**: High-end cinematic shot of a young mother in a bright, modern clothing boutique. She is holding a beautiful children's coat in one hand and her smartphone in the other. On the screen, a clean app with a gold and navy interface displays a "Perfect Match" checkmark. Soft sunlight, 4k, professional color grading, shallow depth of field.
